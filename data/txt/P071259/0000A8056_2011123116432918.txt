                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The implementation of the Zambia Water Sector Performance Improvement Project (WSPIP) is proceeding in a satisfactory manner. Additional Financing for the Project in an amount
                         of SDR 6.5 million (US$ 10 million) became effective as of March 29, 2011. The Additional Financing finances rehabilitation activities for water supply systems in the towns of Kafue,
                         Chongwe, and Luangwa, which are operated by LWSC).
