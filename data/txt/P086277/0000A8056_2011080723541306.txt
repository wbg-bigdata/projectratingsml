                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The revised Project Development Objectives are to: (i) upgrade and rehabilitate the secondary and local roads network; and (ii) increase RDMRDI's and local
                         governments' capacity to manage the road network in a cost effective and sustainable manner.
