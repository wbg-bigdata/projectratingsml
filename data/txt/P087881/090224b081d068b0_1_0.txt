                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         To generate short-term employment while addressing urgent rural irrigation, drainage and water infrastructure rehabilitation needs.This will be achieved through a
                         flexible program of labor-intensive civil works to restore and improve rural water supply and irrigation infrastructure.
