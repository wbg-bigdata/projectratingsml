                            RE:     Loan No. 7522-GU (Additional Financing for the
                                    Third Integrated Financial Management Technical Assistance Project)

                            First Amendment to the Loan Agreement and to the Additional
                                        Financing Implementation Letter

            Excellency:

                    Please refer to the Loan Agreement (the “Loan Agreement?) and to the Additional
            Financing Implementation Letter (the “Additional Financing Implementation Letter?), both
            between the Republic of Guatemala (the “Borrower?) and the International Bank for
            Reconstruction and Development (the “Bank?), dated March 6, 2009, for the above-captioned
            Project. Capitalized terms used in this Amendment Letter and not defined herein have the
            meaning ascribed to them in the Loan Agreement.

                    We are pleased to inform you that pursuant to your letters of request dated December 28,
            2010 and June 24, 2009, the Bank hereby agrees to amend the Loan Agreement so as to: (i) adjust
            certain activities in the Project description in Schedule 1 to the Loan Agreement; (ii) revise
            certain indicators in the Additional Financing Implementation Letter; and (iii) formalize the
            change in the institutional arrangements to reflect the amendment to the Administrative
            Arrangement dated June 24, 2009.

                    Consequently the Loan Agreement is hereby amended as follows:

            1.       Section 3.01 of the Loan Agreement is hereby amended to reflect the role of CGO, to
            read as follows:

                             “Section 3.01. The Borrower declares its commitment to the objective of the
                    project, and to this end, shall: (a) carry out Parts A, B, D, E and F of the Project through
                    MOF; and (b) cause CGO to carry out Part C of the Project, all in accordance with the
                    Administrative Arrangement, with due diligence and efficiency and in conformity with
                    appropriate administrative, financial management and technical practices, and shall
                    provide, as promptly as needed, the funds, facilities, services and other resources required
                    for the Project.?
                                              -2-



2.       Section 3.02 of the Loan Agreement is hereby amended to reflect the role of CGO, to
read as follows:

               “Section 3.02. Without limitation upon the provisions of Section 3.01 of this
       Agreement, and except as the Borrower and the Bank shall otherwise agree, the Borrower
       shall and shall cause CGO to ensure that the Project is carried out in accordance with the
       provisions of Schedule 2 to this Agreement.?

3.       Part A.1 (E) of the Project in Schedule 1 to the Loan Agreement is hereby amended to
read as follows:

               “(E) support the adoption of international standards from the International
               Monetary Fund’s Government Finance Statistics Standards Manual (GFSM),
               2001;?

4.    The paragraph in Part D of the Project in Schedule 1 to the Loan Agreement is hereby
amended to read as follows:

                “Provision of technical assistance, training and equipment to strengthen the
       Borrower’s public investment system by supporting a conceptual analysis of SNIP and its
       integration/coordination with SIAF.?

5.    The paragraph in Part E of the Project in Schedule 1 to the Loan Agreement is hereby
amended to read as follows:

               “Development and piloting of a comprehensive training plan for public servants
       involved in planning, monitoring and evaluation, financial management, public
       procurement, human resource management and auditing areas, taking into consideration
       the current needs and requirements under the SIAF model and according to international
       best practices in these areas, including extensive use of e-learning.?

6.      Section I.A.2 of Schedule 2 to the Loan Agreement is hereby amended to include the role
of the CGO, to read as follows:

       “2.     Without limitation to the provisions of paragraph 1 of this Section, the Borrower,
               through MOF shall, and shall cause CGO, to carry out the Project in accordance
               with the Project Operational Manual and on the basis of the indicators set forth in
               the Additional Financing Implementation Letter. In case of any conflict between
               the terms of this Agreement and those of the Project Operational Manual, those
               of this Agreement shall prevail.?

7.       Section II.A.1 of Schedule 2 to the Loan Agreement is hereby amended to include the
role of the CGO, to read as follows:

       “1.     The Borrower, in coordination with CGO, shall monitor and evaluate the
               progress of the Project and prepare Project Reports in accordance with the
               provisions of Section 5.08 of the General Conditions and on the basis of the
               monitoring indicators agreed with the Bank. Each Project Report shall cover the
               period of one calendar semester, and shall be furnished to the Bank no later than
               one month after the end of the period covered by such report.?
                                                -3-



8.      Section II.B of Schedule 2 to the Loan Agreement is hereby amended to include the role
of the CGO, to read as follows:

        “1.     The Borrower, through MOF, shall and shall cause CGO to maintain or cause to
                be maintained a financial management system in accordance with the provisions
                of Section 5.09 of the General Conditions.

        2.      Without limitation on the provisions of Part A of this Section, the Borrower,
                through MOF, shall, in coordination with CGO, prepare and furnish to the Bank
                not later than forty-five (45) days after the end of each calendar semester, interim
                unaudited financial reports for the Project covering the semester, in form and
                substance satisfactory to the Bank.

        3.      The Borrower, through MOF, shall, in coordination with CGO, have its Financial
                Statements audited in accordance with the provisions of Section 5.09 of the
                General Conditions. Each audit of the Financial Statements shall cover the
                period of one fiscal year of the Borrower. The audited Financial Statements for
                each such period shall be furnished to the Bank not later than six months after the
                end of such period.?

9.    The definition of Additional Financing Implementation Letter in paragraph 1 of the
Appendix to the Loan Agreement is hereby amended to read as follows:

        “1.     Additional Financing Implementation Letter? means the letter dated March 6,
                2009 from the Borrower to the Bank setting forth the Project monitoring
                indicators referred to in Section II.A.1 of Schedule 2 to this Agreement, as
                amended.?

10.   The definition of Administrative Arrangement in paragraph 2 of the Appendix to the
Loan Agreement is hereby amended to read as follows:

        “2.     Administrative Arrangement? means the contractual arrangement No. 4-95 dated
                April 6, 1995, entered into between MOF and CGO which stipulates, inter alia,
                the terms under which MOF and CGO have agreed to coordinate the execution of
                the Project, as amended on June 24, 2009.

11.     The definition of SEGEPLAN in paragraph 15 of the Appendix to the Loan Agreement is
hereby deleted in its entirety.

12.     The Additional Financing Implementation Letter is hereby amended to replace its Annex
with the Attachment to this amendment letter.

         Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing amendment by signing and dating this
letter in the spaces provided below. All other provisions of the Loan Agreement except as
amended herein, shall remain in full force and effect. This amendment letter shall be executed in
two counterparts, each of which shall be an original.

forth in this amendment letter shall become effective as of the date of this letter upon receipt by

satisfactory to the Bank, that the Project Operational Manual has been updated in a manner
satisfactory to the Bank.

       Please note that the restructuring paper dated March 1, 2011 will be disclosed on the
