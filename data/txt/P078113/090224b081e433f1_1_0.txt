                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project is expected to close on August 31, 2013. The overall achievements of the project at this time is moderately satisfactory. Many of the project outputs have also been
                         achieved. This can be seen by the fact that: (i) all Community-Based Contracting (CBC) for school construction has been completed; (ii) all community Grants were provided to the
                         communities; (iii) the teacher training project activities have been successful completed with 503 teachers completing the 16 weeks upgrading program over 2 years during vacation;
Public Disclosure Copy




                         (iv) all text-book procurement has been completed; (v) the student assessment activities were completed; (vi) activities under the strengthening policy and strategy development sub-
                         component were implemented as design; (vii) the information system has been strengthened to the extent that annual yearbooks are being produced and posting in MOES website;
                         and (viii) the tracking study of National University of Lao graduates has been completed with the first intake of Education Administration and Management Division students. The CBC
                         of the 47 additional school have been completed.
