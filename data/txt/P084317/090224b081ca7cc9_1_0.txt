                         Implementation Status Overview
                         During the overall implementation period, the project was successful in increasing access and gender equity in primary education. It was less successful in improving quality and
                         efficiency of primary education. From 2005 until now (preliminary data from 2011 – the most recent data available at this time), primary completion rate improved from 50% to 80.6%
                         (target of 90%); gross enrollment rate improved from 107% to 120.7% (target of 114%); gender parity improved from 91% to 106% (target of 95%); repetition rate improved from 30%

                         Key Decisions Regarding Implementation
                         The Congolese education authorities expressed their commitment in continuing to work towards improving the timely production of education statistics, as well as to take the
                         necessary measures to support the completion of activities under Component 2 that may not be concluded by June 30, 2013.

                         UNDP has expressed its commitment to do all in its means to complete the civil works program and the furbishing of all schools by the closing date of the project.
