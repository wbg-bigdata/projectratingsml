                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The global environmental objective (GEO) of IEM-JO is “to secure the ecological integrity of the Jordan Rift Valley as a globally important corridor?. The PDO of IEM-JPO is “to apply
                         the principles of IEM to the existing land use master plan of the Jordan Rift valley and establish a network of well-managed PAs that meets local ecological, social, and economic

                         established. In addition, there are numerous Memoranda of Understanding (MOU) signed between RSCN and private sector entities for joint management of conservation
                         easements. All 5 Components have been fully delivered Substantial Overall Implementation Progress has being made in the past three months such that the project has achieved
