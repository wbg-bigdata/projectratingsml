PROJECT AGREEMENT
AGREEMENT, dated February 10, 1994, between INTERNATIONAL BANK FOR 
RECONSTRUCTION AND DEVELOPMENT (the Bank) and the STATE OF PARAIBA (PARAIBA);
WHEREAS (A) by the Loan Agreement of even date herewith between Federative 
Republic of Brazil (the Borrower) and the Bank, the Bank has agreed to make available 
to the Borrower an amount in various currencies equivalent to two hundred and six 
million six hundred thousand dollars ($206,600,000), on the terms and conditions set 
forth in the Loan Agreement, but only on condition that PARAIBA agree to undertake 
such obligations toward the Bank as are set forth in this Agreement;
(B)
by a Subsidiary Agreement to be entered into between the Borrower and 
PARAIBA, a portion of the proceeds of the loan provided for under the Loan Agreement 
will be transferred to PARAIBA on the terms and conditions set forth in said 
Subsidiary Agreement; and
WHEREAS PARAIBA, in consideration of the Bank's entering into the Loan Agreement 
with the Borrower, has agreed to undertake the obligations set forth in this 
Agreement;
NOW THEREFORE the parties hereto hereby agree as follows:
ARTICLE I
Definitions

in the preamble to this Agreement, the Loan Agreement and the General Conditions (as 
so defined) have the
respective meanings therein set forth.
ARTICLE II
Execution of the Project
Section 2.01. PARAIBA declares its commitment to the objectives of the Project 
as set forth in Schedule 2 to the Loan Agreement, and, to this end, shall carry out 
its part of the Project
with due diligence and efficiency and in conformity with appropriate administrative, 
financial, engineering and educational practices, and shall provide, or cause to be 
provided, through the agreements referred to in Section 2.10 of this Agreement, 
promptly as needed, the funds, facilities, services and other resources required for 
the carrying out of its part of the Project.
Section 2.02. Except as the Bank shall otherwise agree, procurement of the 
goods, works and consultants' services required for the Project and to be financed out 
of the proceeds of the Loan
shall be governed by the provisions of Schedule 4 to the Loan Agreement.
Section 2.03. PARAIBA shall carry out the obligations set forth in Sections 
9.04, 9.05, 9.06, 9.07, 9.08 and 9.09 of the General Conditions (relating to 
insurance, use of goods and
services, plans and schedules, records and reports, maintenance and land acquisition, 
respectively) in respect of its part of the Project.
Section 2.04. PARAIBA shall duly perform all its obligations under the 
Subsidiary Agreement. Except as the Bank shall otherwise agree, PARAIBA shall not take 
or concur in any action which would have the effect of amending, abrogating, assigning 
or waiving the Subsidiary Agreement or any provision thereof.
Section 2.05. PARAIBA shall provide to the Borrower all information necessary to 
allow the Borrower to comply with its obligations under Sections 3.06, 3.07 and 3.08 
of the Loan Agreement
in a timely manner, such information to include, inter alia:
(a)
an assessment of the progress made by PARAIBA in achieving its AMET 
during the preceding year, by not later than April 30 of each year, starting on April 
30, 1994;
(b)
a proposed PAA for the subsequent twelve-month period starting on January 
1, by not later than August 31 of each year, starting on August 31, 1994; and
(c)
an assessment of the progress made by PARAIBA in the execution of its 
part of the Project, by not later than January 31 and July 31 of each year, of such 
scope and in such detail as the Borrower and the Bank shall reasonably request.
Section 2.06. PARAIBA shall:
(a)
by not later than June 30 of each year, starting on June 30, 1994, 
excepting in 1995, review, jointly with the Borrower and the Bank, the progress in the 
execution of its part of the Project, any such review to be based upon the progress 
reports furnished by the Borrower to the Bank pursuant to Section 3.07 (a) of the Loan 
Agreement; and
(b)
take or cause to be taken all such remedial action which shall have been 
agreed upon by the Bank, the Borrower and the Participating States during the review 
referred to in paragraph (a) above, as necessary for the efficient execution of the 
Project or the achievement of its objectives, such action to be taken in the manner 
and within the timetable agreed upon during such review.
Section 2.07. PARAIBA shall:
Page  3
(a)
by not later than June 30, 1995 review, jointly with the Borrower and the 
Bank, the progress in the carrying out of its part of the Project, the performance of 
its respective obligations under this Agreement, and the Subsidiary Agreement, and on 
the achievement of the objectives of the Project, such review to be based on the terms 
of reference referred to in paragraph (a) of Section 3.08 of the Loan Agreement; and
(b)
take or cause to be taken all such remedial action which shall have been 
agreed upon by the Bank, the Borrower and the Participating States during the review 
referred to in paragraph (a) above, as necessary for the efficient execution of the 
Project or the achievement of its objectives, such action to be taken in the manner 
and within the timetable agreed upon during such review.
Section 2.08. PARAIBA shall promptly inform the Bank and the Borrower of any 
condition which interferes or threatens to interfere with the progress of the carrying 
out of its part of the Project, the accomplishment of the purposes of the Loan, or the 
performance by PARAIBA of its obligations under this Agreement and under the 
Subsidiary Agreement.
Section 2.09. PARAIBA shall maintain UESP, until completion of the Project, as 
referred to in Section 6.01 (d) of the Loan Agreement.
Section 2.10. (a) PARAIBA shall enter into an agreement (convenio) with each 
Eligible Municipality, on terms and conditions satisfactory to the Bank, such 
agreement to provide, inter alia:
(i)
the mechanisms to make available to such Eligible Municipality 
part of the 
proceeds of the Loan to be disbursed under Categories 
(1) and (2) of the table 
set forth in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the 
Loan Agreement, with respect to 
the carrying out of Municipal 
Subprojects;
(ii)
that the respective Eligible Municipality shall:
 
(A)
allocate for education expenditures, in its annual budget, 
no less than 
25% of its annual revenues originating from taxes 
(including tax 
revenues transferred from other 
sources);
(B)
carry out the corresponding PAI and cause each Subproject to 
be carried 
out in a manner satisfactory to the Bank;
 
(C)
provide all information requested by PARAIBA, in order to 
comply with 
its obligations under Sections 2.05 and 2.07 of this 
Agreement;
(D)
not hire any new education staff without a competitive 
examination (concurso);
 
(E)
only make new investments in school refurbishment, expansion 
and 
construction which are consistent with its 
prioritized program of 
investments, duly reviewed by 
PARAIBA, based on a municipal school 
mapping survey 
prepared in accordance with the criteria set forth in 
the 
Operational Manual; and
(F)
provide all funds necessary to finance the operation and 
maintenance of 
the municipal schools; and
 
(iii)
that the works and goods to be considered as Subproject Eligible 
Expenditures 
shall be procured in accordance with the provisions of 
Schedule 4 to the Loan 
Agreement.
 
(b)
Unless the Bank shall otherwise agree, PARAIBA shall not abrogate, amend, 
waive or fail to comply with any such convenio or any provision thereof, and shall 
exercise its rights under any such convenio in such manner as to protect the interests 
of the Borrower and the Bank and to accomplish the purposes of the Loan.

ARTICLE III
Financial Covenants
Section 3.01 (a) PARAIBA shall maintain or cause to be maintained records and 
separate accounts adequate to reflect in accordance with sound accounting practices 
the operations, resources and expenditures in respect of its part of the Project of 
the departments or agencies of PARAIBA responsible for carrying out its part of the 
Project.
(b)
PARAIBA shall:
 
(i)
have the records and accounts referred to in paragraph (a) of this 
Section for 
each fiscal year audited, in accordance with appropriate 
auditing principles 
consistently applied, by independent auditors 
acceptable to the Bank;
(ii)
furnish to the Bank as so available, but in any case not later 
than six months 
after the end of each such year, the report of such 
audit by said auditors, of 
such scope and in such detail as the 
Bank shall have reasonably requested; and
(iii)
furnish to the Bank such other information concerning said records 
and 
accounts and the audit thereof as the Bank shall from time 
to time reasonably 
request.
 
ARTICLE IV
Effective Date; Termination;
Cancellation and Suspension
Section 4.01. This Agreement shall come into force and effect on the date upon 
which the Loan Agreement becomes effective.
Section 4.02. This Agreement and all obligations of the Bank and of PARAIBA 
thereunder shall terminate on the date on which the Loan Agreement shall terminate in 
accordance with its terms, and the Bank shall promptly notify PARAIBA thereof.
Section 4.03. All the provisions of this Agreement shall continue in full force 
and effect notwithstanding any cancellation or suspension under the General 
Conditions.
