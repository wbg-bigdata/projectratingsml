                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project's interventions in school optimization and per capita financing have jump started nationwide education financing reform and led to the preparation of a follow up IDA
                         operation approved by the Board in January 2013. The activities planned under the original project have been completed and those supported by the Additional Financing are on
                         track and expected to be finalized by October 2013.
