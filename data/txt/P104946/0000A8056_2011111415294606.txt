                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The objectives of the project are to: (a) provide quality maternal care to about 30,000 eligible women in targeted districts in Sana'a and (b) design and implement
                         a model of maternal care which demonstrates how Yemeni public policy on maternal and child care canbe effectively integrated with private health provision.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Enrollment and delivery: As of June 30, 2011, 8,180 and 5,697 women enrolled and gave births, respectively, compared to 6,127 and 4,082 enrolled and gave births at the end of
                         December 2010.

                         Education and public awareness campaigns: From January to June 2011,372 maternal health education sessions were conducted and attended by 2,158 women (total number of
                         beneficiaries receiving education services). Health education sessions have achieved results that were above and beyond targets as SOUL continued to conduct health outreach
                         sessions at the clinics and distributed health messages and materials on #mother and child health# to beneficiaries.
