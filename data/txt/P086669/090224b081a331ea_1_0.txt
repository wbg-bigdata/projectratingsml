                         Implementation Status Overview
                         This ISR reflects the findings of the implementation support visit conducted from March 11 to 15, 2013.

                         There is noticeable progress in the implementation of key project activities contributing to the achievement of the Project Development Objectives. Twenty-one Emergency Medical
                         Services (EMS) Centers are now fully functional since November 2012 and two additional hospital EMS Departments and Emergency Services have been established, reaching 9
                         (target achieved). An additional 12 EMS Departments and Emergency Services will receive equipment by July 2013. The procurement of telemedicine equipment has also been
