                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Project implementation is satisfactory with 94% of all the resources from the loan already disbursed. All rehabilitation works have been completed (approx. 1100 km), and are
                         currently being maintained through performance-based arrangements. SEINFRA, DERBA and SEI Project institutional strengthening activities have started to deliver results.
