                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The proposed Project will support the Department of Antioquia's long-term vision to improve the employability of young people a nd increase the competitiveness of the
                         labor force by: (i) increasing equitable access and retention in upper-secondary education, (ii) improving equitable educational achievement in upper-secondary
                         education, and (iii) increasing the proportion of public school secondary-education graduates who continue on to tertiary education.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project has maintained a sustained rhythm of implementation. Under the previous administration the project was used officially as the main channel for improving upper-

                         secondary education and the transition to tertiary education in Antioquia. Some important results were achieved during the last period such as the increase in upper secondary
                         education completion rates in Antioquia's 25 poorest municipalities from 81 to 93 percent, increase in net enrollment in these poorest municipalities from 22 to 34.8 percent., all 451
                         upper secondary schools received the training and have developed a new competency-based curricula, and are now equipped with ICT hardware and software. According to a 2011
                         survey principals, teachers, students and parents recognize the benefit of the project.
