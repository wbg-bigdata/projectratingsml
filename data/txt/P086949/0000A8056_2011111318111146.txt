                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The Project was restructured in September 2011 (level 2) to (i) extend the closing date from September 30, 2011 to September 25, 2012 to allow completion of ongoing project
                         activities, and (ii) cancel an amount of Euro 3.5 million from the Loan. This is the second project restructuring, after the one done in September 2010 to (i) align the Project
