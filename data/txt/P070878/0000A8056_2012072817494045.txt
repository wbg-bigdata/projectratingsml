                         Implementation Status Overview
                         An implementation support and supervision mission was conducted in Guinea from June 18 to 23, including a two day field visits to assess implementation progress of NRM Income
                         Generating Activities (IGAs) and Micro-Projects (MPs). IGAs and MPs constitute the bulk of activities to be implemented before project end in December 2013, in addition to capacity
                         building activities of the Ministry of Environment for the management of Marine Protected Areas created by the project. Overall, activities are progressing well: (i) All 11 rural
