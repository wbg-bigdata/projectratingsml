                                   Fifth Amendment to the Grant Agreement

        Excellency:

                 We refer to the Grant Agreement (“Agreement?) for a Multi-donor Trust Fund (“MDTF?)
        for the Co-financing of the General Education Quality Improvement Project in Ethiopia
        (TF094224) dated July 21, 2009, and countersigned on July 24, 2009, and as amended pursuant to
        the letter dated June 3, 2010, and countersigned on June 4, 2010, amended again pursuant to the
        letter dated July 21, 2010, and countersigned on July 26, 2010, amended again pursuant to the
        letter dated October 13, 2010, and countersigned on October 15, 2010, and amended again
        pursuant to the letter dated March 14, 2011 and countersigned on March 17, 2011 between the
        International Development Association (“World Bank?), acting as administrator of grant funds
        provided by various donors under the Multi-donor Trust Fund (“MDTF?) and the Federal
        Democratic Republic of Ethiopia (“Recipient?).

                We are pleased to inform you that the World Bank has received some funds as their
        commitments from the participating donors for the MDTF. Consequently, we propose to amend
        the Agreement in respect of the provisions below:

        1.       First paragraph of the Agreement is amended to read as follows:

        “In response to the request for financial assistance made on behalf of the Federal Democratic
        Republic of Ethiopia (“Recipient?), I am pleased to inform you that the International Development
        Association (“World Bank?), acting as administrator of grant funds provided by various donors
        under the Multi-donor Trust Fund for Co-financing of the General Education Quality Improvement
        Project in Ethiopia, proposes to extend to the Recipient, a grant in an amount not to exceed one
        hundred nineteen million four hundred eighteen thousand eight hundred forty United States Dollars
        (US$119,418,840) (“Grant?) on the terms and conditions set forth or referred to in this letter
        agreement (“Agreement?), which includes the attached Annex, to assist in the financing of the
        project described in the Annex (“Project?). The Project is being co-financed by the World Bank
        through a: (a) Financing Agreement (“Credit No. 45350-ET?), entered into between the Recipient
        and the World Bank on December 29, 2008 for an amount equivalent to Special Drawing Rights
        33,500,000 (“Financing Agreement?); (b) Letter Agreement between the Recipient and the World
        Bank, dated November 12, 2008, and countersigned on November 21, 2008, for a grant of
        US$70,000,000 provided under the associated Education for All – Fast Track Initiative Catalytic
        Fund Grant Agreement (“Grant No. TF093227?); and (c) Letter Agreement between the Recipient
