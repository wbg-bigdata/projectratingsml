                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Following the signature of the retroactive extension in April 2011, the project activities resumed in July 2011. The 2011 -2012 action was prepared by the Government and approved
                         by the Bank in June 2011. Disbursements are expected to pick up again, and are currently at 93 percent. Agreements with NGOs and sector Ministries were signed in September,
