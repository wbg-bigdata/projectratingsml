                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The objective of the Project is to assist the Recipient in its efforts to improve the conditions for teaching and learning in its territory, through: (i) expanded access to
                         education, (ii) enhanced quality of learning, and (iii) strengthened human resource management.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project was suspended due to country circumstances and has been reactivated with the recent restructuring approval in October 2011. The amended grant agreement was
                         signed on November 11, 2011 with a closing date of June 30, 2013. The first supervision mission took place in December 2011 jointly with other donor partners.
