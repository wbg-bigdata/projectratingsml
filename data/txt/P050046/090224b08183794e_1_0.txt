                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The objective of the Project is to assist the Recipient in its efforts to improve the conditions for teaching and learning in its territory, through: (i) expanded access to
                         education, (ii) enhanced quality of learning, and (iii) strengthened human resource management.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Approximately three-fourths (73%) of the AF grant of US $10 million has been disbursed. The third component of the project was revised during project restructuring in October
                         2011and two new sub-components were added to support reforms related to human resource (HR) management. Most of the activities are on track and two of three PDO indicators
                         to measure efforts to reform personnel management have been achieved.
