AGREEMENT, dated        0         9_         , 1987, between
SOMALI DEMOCRATIC REPUBLIC (the Borrower) and the INTERNATIONAL
DEVELOPMENT ASSOCIATION (the Association).
WHEREAS (A) the Borrower, having satisfied itself as to the
feasibility and priority of the Project described in Schedule 2 to
this Agreement, has requested the Association to assist in the
financing of the Project;
(B) the Borrower intends to contract from the African
Development Fund (ADF) a loan (the ADF Loan) in an amount not
exceeding the equivalent of nine million dollars ($9,000,000) to
assist in financing part of the Project on the terms and
conditions set forth in an agreement (the ADF Loan Agreement) to
be entered into between the Borrower and ADF; and
WHEREAS the Association has agreed, on the basis, inter alia,
of the foregoing, to extend the Credit to the Borrower upon the
terms and conditions set forth in this Agreement;
NOW THEREFORE the parties hereto hereby agree as follows:
ARTICLE I
General Conditions; Definitions
Section 1.01. The "General Conditions Applicable to Develop-
ment Credit Agreements" of the Association, dated January 1, 1985,
with the last sentence of Section 3.02 deleted (the General
Conditions) constitute an integral part of this Agreement.
Section 1.02.  Unless the   context otherwise requires, the
several terms defined in the General Conditions and in the
Preamble to this Agreement have the respective meanings therein
set forth and the following additional terms have the following
meanings:
(a) "Special Accounts" mean the accounts referred to in
Section 2.02 (b) of this Agreement;
(b) "Project Preparation Advance" means the project prepara-
tion advance granted by the Association to the Borrower pursuant
to an exchange of letters dated June 18, 1986, and November 28,
1986, between the Borrower and the Association;


﻿-2-
(c) "AFMET" means the Agricultural Farm Management and
Extension Training Unit established pursuant to Presidential
Decree Number 8;
(d) "MOA" means the Borrower's Ministry of Agriculture;
(e) "MOF" means the Borrower's Ministry of Finance;
(f) "MFLR" means the Borrower's Ministry of Forestry,
Livestock and Range; and
(g) "Project Area" comprises eight regions of Lower
Shebelli, Middle Shebelli, Bay, Lower Juba, Middle Juba, Gedo,
Hiran and Bakool in Southern Somalia.
ARTICLE II
The Credit
Section 2.01. The Association agrees to lend to the Borrower,
on the terms and conditions set forth or referred to in this
Agreement, an amount in various currencies equivalent to ten
million one hundred thousand Special Drawing Rights
(SDR 10,100,000).
Section 2.02. (a) The amount of the Credit may be withdrawn
from the Credit Account in accordance with the provisions of this
Section and of Schedule 1 to this Agreement for expenditures made
(or, if the Association shall so agree, to be made) in respect of
the reasonable cost of goods and services required for the Project
described in Schedule 2 to this Agreement and to be financed out
of the proceeds of the Credit.
(b) The Borrower shall, for the purposes of the Project,
open and maintain in dollars two special accounts in its central
bank on terms and conditions satisfactory to the Association. The
first account (the AFMET Special Account) shall be for purposes of
expenditures under Part A of the Project, and the second account
(the Research Directorate Special Account) shall be for
expenditures under Part B of the Project. Deposits into, and
payments out of, the Special Accounts shall be made in accordance
with the provisions of Schedule 5 to this Agreement.
(c) Promptly after the Effective Date, the Association
shall, on behalf of the Borrower, withdraw from the Credit Account
and pay to itself the amount required to repay the principal


﻿- 3 -
amount of the Project Preparation Advance withdrawn and outstand-
ing as of such date and to pay all unpaid charges thereon. The
unwithdrawn balance of the authorized amount of the Project Prep-
aration Advance shall thereupon be cancelled.
Section 2.03. The Closing Date shall be December 31, 1993 or
such later date as the Association shall establish. The Asso-
ciation shall promptly notify the Borrower of such later date.
Section 2.04. (a) The Borrower shall pay to the Association a
commitment charge at the rate of one-half of one percent (1/2 of
1%) per annum on the principal amount of the Credit not withdrawn
from time to time. The commitment charge shall accrue from a date
sixty days after the date of this Agreement to the respective
dates on which amounts shall be withdrawn by the Borrower from the
Credit Account or shall be cancelled.
(b) The commitment charge shall be paid: (i) at such places
as the Association shall reasonably request; (ii) without restric-
tions of any kind imposed by, or in the territory of, the
Borrower; and (iii) in the currency specified in this Agreement
for the purposes of Section 4.02 of the General Conditions or in
such other eligible currency or currencies as may from time to
time be designated or selected pursuant to the provisions of that
Section.
Section 2.05. The Borrower shall pay to the Association a
service charge at the rate of three-fourths of one percent (3/4 of
1%) per annum on the principal amount of the Credit withdrawn and
outstanding from time to time.
Section 2.06. Commitment charges and service charges shall be
payable semiannually on October 1 and April 1 in each year.
Section 2.07. The Borrower shall repay the principal amount
of the Credit in semiannual installments payable on each October 1
and April 1 commencing October 1, 1997, and ending April 1, 2037.
Each installment to and including the installment payable on April
1, 2007 shall be one-half of one percent (1/2 of 1%) of such
principal amount, and each installment thereafter shall be one and
one-half percent (1-1/2%) of such principal amount.
Section 2.08. The currency of the United States of America is
hereby specified for the purposes of Section 4.02 of the General
Conditions.

ARTICLE III
Execution of the Project
Section 3.01. (a) The Borrower declares its commitment to the
objectives of the Project as set forth in Schedule 2 to this
Agreement and, to this end, shall carry out the Project through
its Ministry of Agriculture with due diligence and efficiency and
in conformity with appropriate agricultural and financial
practices, and shall provide, promptly as needed, the funds,
facilities, services and other resources required for the Project.
(b) Without limitation upon the provisions of paragraph (a)
of this Section and except as the Borrower and the Association
shall otherwise agree, the Borrower shall carry out the Project in
accordance with the Implementation Program set forth in Schedule 4
to this Agreement.
Section 3.02. Except as the Association shall otherwise
agree, procurement of the goods, works and consultants' services
required for the Project and to be financed out of the proceeds of
the Credit shall be governed by the provisions of Schedule 3 to
this Agreement.
Section 3.03. The Borrower shall: (a) maintain in operation
the Project Management Unit (PMU) established under the
Agriculture Extension and Farm Management Training Project (Credit
905-SO) with the same membership as provided therein which shall
assist the Borrower in carrying out Part A of the Project; and (b)
maintain in employment, during execution of the Project, the
General Manager, Technical Manager and Financial Controller of
PMU, whose qualification and experience shall be acceptable to the
Association.
Section 3.04. In order to assist the Borrower in carrying out
Part B of the Project, the Borrower shall: (a) maintain in
operation until the completion of the Project, the Research
Directorate established in MOA which shall be responsible for
coordinating all research activities under the Project and
identifing areas requiring the application of adaptive research
methods; and (b) maintain in employment, during executions of the
Project, the Director General of Research whose qualifications and
experience shall be acceptable to the Association.
Section 3.05. With reference to Section 9.08 of the General
Conditions the Borrower shall furnish to the Association, not
later than June 30, 1987 documentary evidence showing that land
has been allocated for the Project.


﻿-5-
Section 3.06. (a) The Borrower shall or shall cause the prep-
aration and submission to the Association for review and comments,
not later than March 1 of each year, the Annual Work Program and
Budget for the succeeding two agricultural seasons.
(b) The Annual Work Program and Budget referred to in para-
graph (a) of this Section shall include: (i) a review of Project
implementation in the current year; (ii) a detailed description of
work, expressed in terms of key indicators, to be carried out in
the forthcoming two agricultural seasons, including objectives,
deployment of staff and equipment; (iii) assessment of manpower
resources, equipment, fuel and other material resources; (iv) a
budget; and (v) a financing plan including foreign and local
currency requirements and the Borrower's contribution to the
Project.
(c) The Borrower shall or shall cause the implementation of
the Annual Work Program and Budget taking into account the
Association's comments.
Section 3.07. (a) The Borrower shall, not later than
December 31, 1987, establish within MOA a Project Technical
Coordination Committee whose chairman shall be the Vice Minister
of Agriculture and whose other members shall include the General
Manager, AFMET, the Director General of Research, the Research
Advisor, the Directors of research stations, one representative
each from MFLR and MOF, and the Technical Manager of AFMET who
shall be the Secretary of the Committee.
(b) The Project Technical Coordination Committee shall be
responsible for: (i) reviewing Project implementation once every
quarter; (ii) coordinating the research program and extension
linkages; and (iii) coordinating the supply of inputs to farmers.
Section 3.08. Without limitation upon its obligations under
Section 3.01 of this Agreement, the Borrower shall: (a) for the
purposes of the Project, open and thereafter maintain until the
completion of the Project in the Central Bank of Somalia two
Project Advance Accounts to be used for the purposes of meeting
recurrent expenditures for Parts A and B of the Project
respectively; (b) deposit in the said Accounts the sum of Somali
Shillings thirty million (So.Sh. 30,000,000) and Somali Shillings
fifteen million (So.Sh. 15,000,000) respectively; and (c) maintain
at all times a minimum balance of Somali Shillings ten million
(So.Sh. 10,000,000) and five million (So.Sh. 5,000,000) in each
account respectively.

SCHEDULE 2
Description of the Project
The objectives of the Project are to sustain an increase in
agricultural production in the Project Area through strengthening
of the existing agricultural extension and research systems, and
setting up an institutional mechanism for research program
planning and coordination.
The Project consists of the following parts, subject to such
modifications thereof as the Borrower and the Association may
agree upon from time to time to achieve such objectives:
Part A:
1.   Extension
Strengthening the extension services based on the Training
and Visit system in the Project Area through:
(a) the provision of the following at the Afgoi Extension
Headquarters:
(i) additional extension staff;
(ii) support services;
(iii) houses and office buildings;
(iv) vehicles and equipment; and
(v) technical assistance in extension services and
training; and
(b) the provision of the following to the Field Extension
Organizations in the other parts of the Project Area:
(i) additional extension staff,
(ii) equipment and vehicles, and
(iii) operating costs.


﻿- 15 -
2.   Training
(a) Improving training facilities at the Farm Management and
Extension Center at Afgoi and other Extension Training Centers
through the provision of buildings, training facilities,
equipment, vehicles, farm machinery, and operating costs.
(b) Establishment of an Extension Training Center at Yontey.
(c) Provision of technical assistance to the Farm Management
and Extension Training Center for training and for carrying out
farm trials.
(d) Provision of local and overseas training to AFMET staff
and local training for farmers in agriculture research and
extension.
Part B:   Research
Improving MOA's ability to carry out agricultural research
activities through:
1.   The establishment and operation of a Directorate of Research
in MOA through the provision of:
(a) additional research staff;
(b) vehicles and office equipment;
(c) operating costs;
(d) technical assistance; and
(e) materials and equipment for the research records
library.
2.   The maintenance and operation of the Bonka Research Station
through the provision of:
(a) vehicles and equipment;
(b) operating costs;
(c) rehabilitation of buildings; and
(d) technical assistance.


﻿- 16 -
3.   The establishment and maintenance of a Research Support Fund
to finance sub-projects in adaptive research.
The Project is expected to be completed by June 30, 1993.

SCHEDULE 4
Implementation Program
1.   In carrying out the Project, the Borrower shall take the
following measures to ensure that there is a linkage between
extension and research activities:
(a) Before the start of each season, the research stations
shall convene meetings to plan the research and extension programs
for the following season: (i) participants of the research meeting
shall include, inter alia, the Technical Manager, the Director of
Training, the Head of Extension and the Head of Regional Extension
staff, and (ii) participants for the extension meeting shall
include, inter alia, the Technical Manager, the Director of
Training and Senior Regional staff of AFMET.
(b) The meetings shall work out and prepare, inter alia, the
seasonal extension timetable, extension recommendations, monthly
AFMET staff training schedules, research staff participation in
monthly AFMET staff training, and the content and timetable for
seasonal training.
2.   In carrying out the Project, the Borrower shall take
appropriate measures to appoint qualified Somali staff to
understudy each consultant employed under the Project. The
qualification and experience of such staff shall be acceptable to
the Association.
3.   In order to ensure that extension training activities at
Jowhar are not interrupted, the Borrower shall take necessary
mesures to ensure that the Jowhar Extension Training Center
continues to operate from its present premises until such time as
new buildings for the center are completd.
4.   In carrying out Part A of the Project, the Borrower shall
take all appropriate measures to provide the Field Extension
Assistants with new motorcycles. Criteria for selection of Field
Extension Assistants to be provided with motorcycles and the terms
and conditions for allotment of motorcycles shall be acceptable to
the Association.
5.   In carrying out Part A of the Project, the Borrower shall
employ, or shall cause AFMET to employ, not later than June 30,
1988, Somali nationals whose qualifications and experience shall
be acceptable to the Association to assume the posts of (a)
Director of Training and (b) Director of Extension.


﻿- 21 -
6.   In carrying out Part A.2 (b) of the Project, the Borrower
shall: (a) not later than December 31, 1987, take appropriate
measures to transfer the ownership and administration of the
buildings belonging to the Ministry of Education at Yontey to
AFMET; and (b) not later than October 31, 1987, take appropriate
measures to allocate to AFMET land sufficient for the construction
of the Extension Training Center at Jowhar.
7.   In carrying out Part B of the Project in the Bay Region, the
Borrower shall appoint the Management Unit established under the
Bay Region Agriculture Development Project (Credit 972-SO) to
carry out all the research activities at the Bonka Research
Station under procedures acceptable to the Association. Whenever
the Management Unit of the Bay Region Agricultural Development
Project ceases to operate, the responsibility for carrying out
research at Bonka shall be shifted to the Research Directorate of
MOA.
8.   In carrying out Part B of the Project, the Borrower shall
take appropriate measures to employ the Research Advisor who shall
take up his post not later than December 31, 1987.
9.   In carrying out Part B.3 of the Project, the Borrower shall:
(a) adopt criteria, acceptable to the Association, for
selecting sub-projects to be financed under the Research Support
Fund; and
(b) submit to the Association for approval each adaptive
research sub-project to be financed under the Research Support
Fund (i) whose cost is estimated to exceed the equivalent of
$100,000; or (ii) if such sub-project is to be carried out outside
the territory of the Borrower.

SCHEDULE 5
Special Accounts
1.   For the purposes of this Schedule:
(a) the term "eligible Categories" means Categories (1) and
(3) (a) for the AFMET Special Account and Categories (2), (3) (b)
and (4) for the Research Directorate Special Account set forth in
the table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to this Agreement;
(b) the term "eligible expenditures" means expenditures in
respect of the reasonable cost of goods and services required for
the Project and to be financed out of the proceeds of the Credit
allocated from time to time to the respective eligible Categories
in accordance with the provisions of Schedule 1 to this Agreement;
and
(c) the term "Authorized Allocation" means an amount
equivalent to $350,000 for the AFMET Special Account and $200,000
for the Research Directorate Special Account, to be withdrawn from
the Credit Account and deposited into the respective Special
Accounts pursuant to paragraph 3 (a) of this Schedule.
2.   Except as the Association shall otherwise agree, payments out
of the respective Special Accounts shall be made exclusively for
eligible expenditures in accordance with the provisions of this
Schedule.
3.   After the Association has received evidence satisfactory to
it that the Special Accounts have been duly opened, withdrawals of
the Authorized Allocations and subsequent withdrawals to replenish
the respective Special Accounts may be iuade as follows:
(a) On the basis of a request or requests by the Borrower
for deposits which add up to the respective amounts of the
Authorized Allocations, the Association shall, on behalf of the
Borrower, withdraw from the Credit Account and deposit into the
respective Special Accounts such amounts as the Borrower shall
have requested.
(b) The Borrower shall furnish to the Association requests
for replenishment of the respective Special Accounts at such
intervals or in such minimum amounts as the Association shall
specify. On the basis of such requests, the Association shall
withdraw from the Credit Account and deposit into the respective
Special Accounts such amounts as shall be required to replenish
