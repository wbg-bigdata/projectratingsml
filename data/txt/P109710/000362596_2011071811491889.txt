      Dear Ms. Markert / H.E. Governor Hamengku Buwono X / Governor Waluyo:

                                           Re: Indonesia - Java Reconstruction Fund
                              Yogyakarta and Central Java Earthquake: Livelihood Recovery Project
                                     Grant No.TF093853-IND - Extension of Closing Date

              Pursuant to the letter dated May 24, 2011 from Deutsche Gesellschaft für Internationale
      Zusammenarbeit (GIZ) and the endorsement letters from the Government of Yogyakarta Special
      Province and the Government of Central Java Province dated May 23, 2011 and May 25, 2011,
      respectively, requesting us to extend the Closing Date of the above-referenced Grant, I am pleased to
      inform you that the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development / International
      Development Association (collectively referred to as the “World Bank?), acting as administrator of
      grant funds provided by the multi-donor Java Reconstruction Fund concurs with your request.

              Accordingly, the World Bank has established September 30, 2011 as the later date for
      purposes of Schedule 2, Section IV, sub-paragraph B.2 of the Grant Agreement between the
      Government of the Republic of Indonesia, represented by the Yogyakarta Special Region and the
      Central Java Province, GIZ and the World Bank, dated February 23, 2009, as amended to date.
