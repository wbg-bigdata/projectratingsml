                         Implementation Status Overview
                         There has been good progress on the part of rural communities in utilizing natural resources in a more sustainable manner . The original grant funds have been fully disbursed to
                         local governments/communities, and additional financing is now available to provide two more rounds of block grants for “green? sub-projects in the target sub-districts and for
                         accompanying technical assistance to support community planning and implementation

                         Key Decisions Regarding Implementation
                         Project agencies will focus on speeding up implementation of district-level micro-hydro power plants in order to complete all project activities by the December 31, 2012 closing date.
                         A series of studies will be carried out during 2012 to enable the government of Indonesia and its development partners to assess the achievements of the pilot program and decide on
                         the future of PNPM Green.
