                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Implementation is progressing satisfactorily. The Project has already committed all the funds allocated to Alliances plus savings from other activities. The Project has surpassed its
                         target number of alliances financed by 18%, but the total number of direct beneficiaries will be 13% lower than projected due to increases in unitary costs. Were it not for funding
                         constraints, the number of alliances financed could have risen to 1403, 75% above target. The project has exceeded all key indicators of institutional strengthening significantly. There
                         are still some delays in the execution of associated municipal investments but it is expected that most will be finalized before the end of the year.
