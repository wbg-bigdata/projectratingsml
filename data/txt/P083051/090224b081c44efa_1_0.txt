                         Implementation Status Overview
                         This report follows the supervision mission carried out on March 18-22, 2013 and which also included the supervision of the Rural Alliances Project II.

                         The Project has committed all its funds, executed 95% of its budget and its achievements are remarkable. It has financed about 772 alliances (20% over the target) and trained 6,118
                         producers in alliance management (50% over the target). The only delays continue to be found in the implementation of the municipal subproject subcomponent which forms part of
                         some alliances. Support for a total of 386 alliances has concluded; the other 386 have concluded their investment phase and are still receiving support during the operations phase,
                         as planned. The design of the impact evaluation was finalized during the mission. The first survey will be carried out in conjunction with the first call for proposals for Rural Alliances II,
