                         Implementation Status Overview
                         This report follows the supervision mission of May 2012 and the approval of the Rural Alliances Project II by the Bank's Board on October 23,2012.

                         The Project has committed all its funds and its achievements are remarkable. It has financed about 780 alliances (20% over the target) and trained 6,118 producers in alliance
                         management (50% over the target). The only delays are found in the implementation of municipal subprojects which accompany some alliances. An independent evaluation of 140
                         alliances carried out by an FAO team shows an average increase in net income from the alliance product of 158%. Significant increments are reported on the value of sales (79%),
