                         Implementation Status Overview
                         During the review period, the performance of the Technical Implementation Support Consultancy (TISC) has improved with the new team leader and a number of short-term
                         specialists deployed in Kabul. These improvements have resulted in further strengthening of the technical capacity of government counterparts for the project. Considering the
                         improvements made to date, progress towards achievement of the PDO and on implementation progress has been upgraded to “Moderately Satisfactory.?
