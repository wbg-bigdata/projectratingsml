                         Implementation Status Overview
                         During the review period, the performance of the Technical Implementation Support Consultancy (TISC) has been steadily improving with some milestone reports/outputs such as
                         draft knowledge base and river basin atlas for Kabul and Panj-Amu river basins submitted. These improvements have resulted in further strengthening of the technical capacity of
                         government counterparts for the project. Considering the improvements made to date, progress towards achievement of the PDO and on implementation progress has been kept at
                         “Moderately Satisfactory.?
