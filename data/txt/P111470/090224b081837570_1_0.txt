                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The task team has almost completed the restructuring of this project (level 2), which was sent for RVP approval on November 26, 2012. To date, the project has disbursed 64
                         percent of total project funding since the Project was made effective on October 11, 2011. Implementation Progress (IP) rating is being upgraded to Satisfactory given the substantial
                         progress that has been made since the project became effective.

                         The decision to restructure the project was made in order to : (i) narrow the scope of the Government’s activities in the education sector following KfW’s decision to exit the pooled
