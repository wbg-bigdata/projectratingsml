             GHANA: Education Sector Project
               Second Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement (Credit No. 3865-GH)


                We refer to the Development Credit Agreement (the Agreement) for the
        above-mentioned Project, dated March 16, 2004, between the Republic of Ghana (the Borrower)
        and the International Development Association (the Association), as amended by a letter by the
        Association dated August 26, 2009 and counter signed by the Borrower on September 15, 2009
        (the Amendment Letter). We also refer to your letters dated June 18, 2010, requesting for
        amendment to the Agreement, including, inter alia, amendment to reduce the total amount of the
        Credit.
                       We are pleased to inform you that the Association accedes to your request and
        proposes to amend the Agreement as follows:

        1.      Section 1.02 of Article I is amended to read as follows: By inserting new paragraphs (v)
        and (w) respectively, to read as follows:

                                  “(v) “Original Credit Amount? means an amount of SDR 52,700,000
                                 referred to in Section 2.01 of this Agreement; and?

                                 “(w) “Reduced Credit Amount? means an amount of (SDR 4,450,400)
                                 referred to in Section 2.01 of this Agreement.?

        2.      Section 2.01 is amended to read as follows:

         “The Association agrees to lend to the Borrower, on the terms and conditions set forth or referred
        to in this Agreement, an amount in various currencies equivalent to Forty eight million Two
        hundred forty nine thousand and six hundred Special Drawing Rights (SDR 48,249,600) (the
        “Credit?) which consists of an amount in various currencies equivalent to Fifty two million seven
        hundred thousand Special Drawing Rights (SDR 52,700,000) (“Original Credit Amount?), as
        reduced by an amount in various currencies equivalent to Four million Four hundred Fifty
        thousand and Four hundred Special Drawing Rights (SDR 4,450,400) (“Reduced Credit
        Amount?).?
