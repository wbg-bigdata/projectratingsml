  Sudharani is from Old Chintalapudi Village in West Godavari District. She could not complete her college
  education as her father an agricultural laborer could not afford her college fees. Her mother, an SHG member
  told her about EGMM English and computer centers. After three months training she was placed at Big Bazaar,
  the largest retail chain in the country earning a salary of Rs. 3500 per month as a customer service associate.
  Recently, she returned to her home district to work as a cashier with Coromandel Fertilizers which brings in Rs.
  5,000 per month. Sudharani says, “Now I will complete my graduation from the university.? She enrolled both
  her brothers in the EGMM Academy. Today the three working youths have enhanced their family income from
  Rs. 10,000 year to Rs. 144,000 a year.
