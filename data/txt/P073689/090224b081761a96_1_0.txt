                         Implementation Status Overview
                         All of the remaining project activities are expected to be completed before the expected project closing date of December 31, 2012. More specifically:
                         (i) The contract for the construction and rehabilitation of 3 bridges on the RN13 should be completed by December 10, 2012;
                         (ii) the construction of seven bridges on the RN44 and three additional ones is expected to be completed by mid-November, 2012;
                         (iii) the construction of jetties (in 5 lots), to provide for safe and smooth decking/mooring of the APL3-financed ferries, should be completed before mid-November, 2012 (some lots
