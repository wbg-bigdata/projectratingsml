            Excellency,

            We hereby refer to (i) the Community-Based Land Management Project Global
            Environment Facility Grant Agreement (“Grant Agreement?) dated November 7, 2006
            between the Republic of Guinea (“Recipient?) and the International Development
            Association (“Association?), acting as Implementing Agency of the Global Environment
            Facility, in respect of the above-captioned project; and (ii) your letter dated May 18,
            2011.

            We are pleased to inform you that the Association concurs with your request, and
            accordingly, the Association proposes to make the following amendments:


            1. Section I.B of Schedule 2 of the Grant Agreement is hereby amended by inserting the
            following new clause at the end thereof:

                    “ 3. The Project Implementation Manual shall be amended by not later than
                    July 15, 2011 to reflect the revised satisfactory Project indicators referenced in
                    Section II.A.1 of this Agreement.?

            2. Section II.A.1 of Schedule 2 of the Grant Agreement is hereby amended as follows:

                    “ 1. The Recipient shall monitor and evaluate the progress of the Project and
                    prepare Project Reports in accordance with the provisions of Section 2.06 of the
                    Standard Conditions and on the basis of satisfactory indicators set forth in the
                    Project Implementation Manual. Each Project Report shall cover the period of
                    one (1) calendar semester, and shall be furnished to the World Bank not later than
                    one (1) month after the end of the period covered by such report.?
