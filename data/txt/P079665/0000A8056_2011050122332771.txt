                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Component 1 ➢? Policy development and capacity building ➢? This component makes some progress since mid-term review. It is a main vehicle for sourcing technical assistance
                         to support policy development in higher education for the Ministry. Weak areas remain for higher education financing, quality assurance and Higher Education Management
                         Information system.

                         Component 2 - Training Research Innovation Grants - The TRIG component shows substantial progress since the mid-term review.
                         Eighteen months into the project closing, the majority procurement activities have been completed. Of the total 103 contracts, 47 contracts (45% in dollar terms) have been
