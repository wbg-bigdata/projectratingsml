                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project is on track to meet its development objectives and is due to close on time, June 30, 2014. Employment and Self-Employment Services, which includes training services,
                         micro-small business services and employment/job placement services, have been provided to beneficiaries, covering 54 districts. In addition, cash payments to at least 14,200
                         families and 4,620 widows of those killed in the conflict have been provided under the project, surpassing the set targets. Up to 87 percent of the beneficiaries are satisfied with the
