                         Implementation Status Overview
                         GOE has built 8,005 Kindergarten (KG) classrooms nationwide since the beginning of the project of which 3,987 classrooms (vs. 994 targeted) in the 165 project districts. This is in
                         addition to 369 classrooms completed to date under the Bank loan under the public KG classroom component out of 750 targeted and 402 Non-Government Organizations (NGO)
                         classrooms completed under the NGO component out of 500 targeted under the loan.
