                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The Program Development Objective is to enhance regional integration among the riparian countries of the Senegal River Basin through OMVS for multi-purpose water
                         resources development to foster improved community livelihoods.
                         The Project Phase 1 Development Objective is to improve management and use of water resources in the Senegal River Basin.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The mission visit concluded that the overall implementation progress (IP) rating should remain satisfactory. This rating is based on the following: (i) achievement of most of the
                         milestones as set for June 2012; (ii) a current disbursement level of 72%; and (iii) adequate compliance with World Bank fiduciary and safeguards requirements. The Project also
