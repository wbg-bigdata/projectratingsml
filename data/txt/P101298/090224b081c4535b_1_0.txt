                         Implementation Status Overview
                         This report was prepared following an implementation support mission on March 2013.

                         Project implementation is progressing as planned with 85% of the IDA credit and 75% of the total budget (including co-financiers) disbursed, while almost the totality of the available
                         funds have been committed. Both implementing agencies have substantially executed key actions on the critical path towards the scheduled Closing Date of the credit (November
                         2013).
