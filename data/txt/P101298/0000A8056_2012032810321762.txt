                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Project implementation is progressing nicely with 62% of the IDA credit disbursed, 85% of entire Project budget committed. The portfolio consists of a total of 664 investment
                         subprojects under implementation and completed and 256 committed worth a total of US$ 41.5 million.

                         There have been 6 cases discovered of collusion and potential corruption of firms related to proposals for the provision of works on some subprojects in Santa Cruz, 2 cases
                         involving the same company falsifying guarantees in Tarija and 3 potential cases of collusion between firms related to proposals for the provision of 3 roads subprojects in Beni.
