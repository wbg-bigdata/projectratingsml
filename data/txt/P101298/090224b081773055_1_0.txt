                         Implementation Status Overview
                         This report was prepared following an implementation support mission on May 2012 and the approval of the Project restructuring by the Bank's Board on July 30, 2012.

                         The restructuring included a minor adjustment in the Project Development Objective to reflect changes in Bolivia's territorial organization as established in its new Constitution and the
                         Autonomies and Decentralization Law. In addition, the restructuring triggered the Involuntary Resettlement Safeguard Policy to ensure adequate management of possible minor land
                         acquisition during implementation of investment subprojects. Project implementation is progressing as planned with 70% of the IDA credit disbursed and more than 90% of the entire


                                                                                                                                                                                                          Page 1 of 14

                         Project budget committed. The supervision mission agreed on critical paths for both the Institutional Strengthening and Productive Investment components to ensure the Project
                         closes on time; however, the Government will likely request an extension of the Closing Date for the Productive Investments component given that delays in the approval of the
                         Project's restructuring affected subproject assessment and approval by FPS. The design of the Project's evaluation was initiated in May 2012 and will be finalized by November 2012.
