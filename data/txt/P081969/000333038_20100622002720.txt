Overview of existing IT used in Agrani Ba.nk Limited

Agrani Bank Limited is a leading commercial bank in Bangladesh with its branche~: across the country.
Presently the Bank is having a three-tier structure with Head Office at Dhaka, '7 Circle Offices, 52
Zonal Offices and 866 branches all over Bangladesh. Agrani Bank limited also has two subsidiary
companies, viz., Agrani Exchange House, Singapore and Agrani Remittance Home, Malaysia engaged
in serving non-resident Bangladeshi nationals D)r foreign remittance.
Agralli Bank limited's 241 branches are partially computerised with various applications supported by
local databases in LAN environment. These applications are running on platforms like COBOL/OS
400, Oracle/VB and FoxPro. Remaining brar:ches spread out across the country are under manual
operations.
Currently, the following major functions are computerised at the Head office by means of COBOL-
based applications under IBM AS-400 midrange computer platform.
· Consolidation of financial statements of all partially computerised and manually operated
    branches
· Inter- branch reconciliation of fund transfer
· HR and pay roll
Agrani Bank Limited has presently 19 off-line ATMs (e-cash) running at various locations in Dhaka
and Chittagong under a shared ATM switch. Also, the bank provides off-line debit card product
'Ready cash' cards are used for making payment in selected shops in Bangladesh.
SWIFT application and connectivity is available to 14 branches of Agrani Bank Limited through the
bank's SWIFT server at the bank's Head office.
