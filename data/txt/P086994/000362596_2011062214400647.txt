        Excellency,

               We refer to the Development Credit Agreement (the Agreement) between the
        Republic of Djibouti (the Borrower) and the International Development Association (the
        Association), dated November 28, 2005, as amended on December 10, 2005 and May 3,
        2006. We also refer to your letter dated March 27, 2011, [and to the understandings
        reached by representatives of the Borrower and the Association to amend of the
        Agreement].

                Consequently, the Agreement is hereby amended as follows:

            1. The proceeds of the Credit as set out in the table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to
               the Agreement are reallocated and the amounts now allocated to the different
               categories are as indicated in the attachment to this amendment letter.

            2. Part A.1 of Schedule 2 to the Agreement is amended to read as follows:

                “1.    Increasing the capacity of existing schools through: (i) the construction,
                equipment and rehabilitation of classrooms, in primary and middle schools in
                urban and rural areas; and (ii) the construction and equipment of a new middle
                school.?

               All the terms and conditions of the Agreement that have not been amended hereby
        remain unchanged and in full force and effect.
