Archaeological Survey Report
We,Henan  Provinciat  Institute  of  Cultural  Relics  and
Archaeology,as assigned by Henan Provincial High Grade Highway
Construction Authority and Henan Prouincial Bureau of Cultural
Relics,cooperated with Luoyang City Administration of Cultural
Relics,Sanmenxia City Bureau of Cultural Retics,Cultural Relics
Administrations of Mengj in County, the Surburb District of Luoyang,
Xinan   County,Yima  City,Nianchi  County,Shanxian  County,Hubin
District   of   Sanmeuxia   City, organized   and   executed   an
archaeoalogical survey along the alignment of the above ptoject
from December 1994 toFebruarYl995. On the basis of available
information,the archaeological survey concentrated on 33 ancient.
sites and graveyards where the expressway cuts through or passes
nearby. The exploration covered an area of 1201650 square meters
and found 10 ancient sites, totalling 115250 square meters>, 8
cemeteries  inctuding  115  tombs; 35 -storage pits of different
periods; 1 palaeolithic locality;wetI and stone tol manufacturing
site of the Yangshao Culture and a large building foundation of
the Warring States Period and Han Dynasties etc..
The area where the expressway ties is an extremely important
ancient route(Yaohan Route) connecting Guanzhong plain and Huabei
Plain and thus a battlefield of different periods. All kinds of
cultural relics are abundant and valuable for the staty of the
history of ancient war, transportation and culturat exchange. To
reduce the damage which the construction wilt bring to cultural
relics  to a minimum, an archaeological  excavation has to be



carried out at ten percent important sites and cemeteries of
above-mentioned,  totalling   11500  square  meters  before  the
construction of the expressway.Henan Provincial Institute of
Cultural Relics and Archaeology is committed,in about 120 to 150
days,to the full completion of archaeological excavation after the
land being acquired and prior to the actual commencement of the
expressway works and will mobilize its manpower and resources
corresponding to the task.
Among  the above-mentioned 33 cultural retics locatities,in
the light of our investigatioanit is known that the following 7
sites are slightly far from the expressway and thus will not be
injured  during  the  construction.They  are  Suqin  (a  famous
politician of the Warring States Period) tomb,Baizhuang site,
Yinghao site,Xiashi walled town,Xiashi graveyard,Xiya site and
Shij iagou graveyard. Even though Yanghe site, Anbeicun site, Nanwang
site ,flekou site at Yima, Wuyaotou site and Xiaojiaokou site are
stightly near to the expressway, however the archaeological
drilling didn't expose cultural remains and the construction will,
not cause  damage. It should  be  pointed  out that  Zhaojiayuan
pataeolithic  locality,although without dritling,must be carried
out in the future.
The next is an discription of the explorations and the future
work plan of the above-mentioned sites.

I.Destricts of Imperial Amusoleums
and Beimang Cemetery of Norther:i We! Dynasty, Luoyang
Known as the capital of nine dynasties,. Luoyang is one of the
seven great ancient capitals in China. A large number of high
officers, more than ten emperors and their relatives of Eastern
Han and Northern Wei Dynasties were buried on Mang mountain,
several miles to the north of Luoyang City. Now, several hundreds
of the earth-mound are standing in this region. As a result of
cooperating with capital constructions, a large number of ancient
tombs have been discovered   by archaeology and cultural relics
organizations   over   years.    During   the   construction   of
Zhengzhou--Luoyang section of Lianbuo National Hihgway in 1991,
Henan Provincial  Institute of Cultural Relics and Archaeology and
Luoyang Archaeoloical Team discovered 133 tombs of different
petiods form western Han to Song Dynasties on the highway to the
east of Chaoyang , Mengj in County and obtained large numbers of
potteries and procetains,  epitaphs,  three-color-glaze Tang ware
and pottery figurines of musicians and dancers. In 1994, Mengjin
county Archaeologicat DrilLing Team surveied and excavated the
construction area of Luoyang Glass Production Group and found 31
tombs of different periods from Han to tang Dynasties, 22 tombs of
modern period.
Lianhuo National Highway is just passing through the south
slope of the mausoleum of xiaowen( Chang Mausoleum ), an emperor
of Northern Wei, Henan Provincial Institute of Cultural Relics and
Archaeology drilled two sections of the highway(KO-K3;K5-K8) and
discovered 54 ancient tombs to a depth of 5-10m. Most of them are
brick-chambered tombs and cavechamber tombs. They reflect the



burial cumtoms of different periods from Western Ban(206BC--24AD)
to Tang Dynasties(618--907AD). Anarchaeological excavation must be
conducted before the commencement of works.
The unearthing area is about 1080 square meters,and the fund
is about 356400 Yuan, RMB. The excavation will last 90 days from
April. to June 1996, under the sponsor of Luoyang ArchaeologicaL
Team.

2.Shuiquangou Site,  Mengjin County
The   site ties to the west of Shuiquangou Village, in
Matun Town, Mengjin County. The poles are from k4 tok4+500,
about 5,000 metres long. Henan Provincial Institute of
Cultural Relics and Archaeology drilled the site where the
proposed expressway passes, covering an area of 25,000 square
metres from January 6 to 26, 1995. The stratum is composed
of:
a). the first layer-the soft cultivated soil to a depth of
0.20--0.40m.
b). the second layer-the hard tight yellow soil to a depth
of o.60-1.50m, containing fragments of bricks, tiles and
potsherds of Han Dynasty.
c). the   third layer-the soft grey soil to a depth of
0.60-0.80m, containing the potsherds of Erlitou Culture.
b). the forth layer-the hard brown grey soil to a depth of
o.40-0.50m, containing the fragments of painted pottery and
burnt clay of Yangshao Culture.
e). the fifth layer-the primary soil layer.
Throuth the drilling, the storage pits and the building
foundation as well as the cultural deposits belong to Yangshao
and Erlitou Cultures were found between centre of SHuiquangou
Village and pole NO.K4 to k4+500,totalling 50Dm tong. A great
quantity of cultural relics scatter on the surface of the
site. The collected articles include: pottery urns with raised
strips, pottery jars impressed cord pattern, i-tripods, pottery
basins with basket pattern,the fragments of painted pottery,
shell implements,stone axes,stone sickles and so on.lt is
clear that the date of this site is from late Yangshao Culture

where as the area southeast to Fucun Village is abundent in
Longshan Culture.Between the abeve-mentioned areas are
cuttural  remains  of  the  Yangshao,Longshan  and  the  Zhou
Dynasty.A Large quantity of culture relics such as pottery
fragments and stone toots can be found on the surface.They
