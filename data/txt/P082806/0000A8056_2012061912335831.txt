                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Since the last supervision mission in October 2011, the implementation of formerly suspended contracts (especially the key ones: road rehabilitation works on RN2, and ISPS
                         compliance works in the port of Toliary) have resumed. Likewise, arrears incurred after the crisis have all been cleared. By the end of June 2012, the project will disburse half of the
