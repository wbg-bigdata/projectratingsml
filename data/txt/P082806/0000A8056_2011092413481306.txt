                         Implementation Status Overview
                         As the Bank Management authorized the resumption of the Credit disbursement in May 2011, the project was able topay contractual arrears related to works, goods, and services
                         delivered under contracts after the event of March 17, 2009, amounting about US$900,000. The disbursement rates of the initial Credit and its additional financing are respectively
