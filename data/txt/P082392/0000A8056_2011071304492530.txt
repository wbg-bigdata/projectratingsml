                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The proposed revised project development objective is to assist the process of modernizing Grenada's public sector with a focus on improving public sector
                         efficiency in service delivery and management, enhancing private sector collaboration in support of developing small businesses, and promoting regionalization
                         initiatives.
