                             (Community Agriculture and Watershed Management Project)
                               Fourth Amendment to Development Financing Agreement

                We refer to the Development Financing Agreement between the Republic of Tajikistan (the
        Recipient) and the International Development Association (the Association), dated July 14, 2004 (the
        Development Financing Agreement) for the above-referenced Project, as amended by the Amendment
        Letters of November 25, 2004, May 25, 2005, and October 9, 2008, respectively. We also refer to your
        request of April 26, 2011 to amend the Development Financing Agreement again.

               We are pleased to inform you that the Association concurs with your request and agrees to amend
        the Development Financing Agreement as follows:

        (1)     A new sub-paragraph (n) is inserted in Section 1.02 of Article 1 of the Development Financing
        Agreement, while the current sub-paragraph (n) now becomes sub-paragraph (o), with the subsequent sub-
        paragraphs re-lettered accordingly, and said new sub-paragraph (n) reads as follows:

                “National Committee for Television and Radio Broadcasting? means the Recipient’s National
                Committee for Television and Radio Broadcasting established pursuant to Decree Number 610 of
                December 28, 2006, or any successor thereto.?

        (2)     Sub-paragraph (t) in Section 1.02 of the Development Financing Agreement is amended to read
        as follows:

                “(t) PMU? means the Project Management Unit, established under the Decree of the President of
        the Recipient No. 216 dated June 26, 1998, which will be responsible for overall coordination and
        management of the Project, including responsibility for overall coordination and management of Project
        procurement activities, as well as to act as the secretariat and executive of the SLSC (as hereafter
        defined), or any successor thereto.?

        (3)     The Closing Date in Section 2.03 of the Development Financing Agreement is amended to read
        April 30, 2012.

         (4)     The table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the Development Financing Agreement is amended to
        read as follows:
