                         Implementation Status Overview
                         As of December 5, 2011, all the scheduled activities in the procurement plan have been completed, disbursement rate stands at 93% and commitments at about 100%. Based on the
                         current implementation status, the project has achieved its development objectives. On March 21, 2011, based on a request from the client, the project was once again restructured in
                         order to proceed with a reallocation of funds from component 3 to components 1. The reallocation of funds from component 3 to 1 led to a reduction in the required counterpart
