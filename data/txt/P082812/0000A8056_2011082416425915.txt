                         Implementation Status Overview
                         During the last four months, disbursement rate has increased from 68% to 84% and commitments currently stand at about 100%. Based on the current implementation status, the
                         PDO is likely to be achieved. In spite of this steady progress, the overall project implementation "MS" rating has been maintained. This is attributed to (i) poor achievement of
                         population indicators due to sub-project sites changes in Libreville and Port-Gentil; and (ii) poor progress on component 3 (development of local SMEs through increased access to
