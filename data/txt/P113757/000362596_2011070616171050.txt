                        SIERRA LEONE: Decentralized Service Delivery Project
                        Amendment to the Financing Agreement (Credit No. 4656-SL)

                 We refer to the Financing Agreement (the Agreement) for the above-mentioned Project,
        dated November 19, 2009, between the Republic of Sierra Leone (the Recipient) and the
        International Development Association (the Association). We also refer to your letter dated
        March 16, 2011, requesting specific amendments to the Agreement.

               We are pleased to inform you that the Association accedes to your request and proposes
        to amend the Agreement in respect of the following provisions.

        1.       Section IV (“Withdrawal of the Proceeds of the Financing?) of Schedule 2 is amended
        to read as follows:

        (a)     The Table in Part A.2 is amended to read as set forth in Annex 1 to this amendment
                letter; and

        (b)     Part B.2 is amended to read as follows: “The Closing Date is June 29, 2012.?

                All other provisions of the Agreement, except as herein amended, shall remain in full
        force and effect.




                Please indicate agreement to the foregoing, on behalf of the Recipient, by
        countersigning and dating the form of confirmation on the two original copies of this letter
provided and returning one original, as countersigned, to us. Upon receipt by the Association of
the copy of this letter countersigned by the Recipient, this amendment shall become effective as
of the date of its countersignature.
