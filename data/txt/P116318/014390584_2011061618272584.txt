        Dear Dr. Khairy,

                 We refer to the Nile Basin Trust Fund Grant Agreement (“Agreement?) between the
        International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (“IBRD?) and the International
        Development Association (“IDA?) (collectively the “Bank?), as administrator of the Nile Basin
        Trust Fund (“NBTF?), and the Nile Basin Initiative (“Recipient?) dated March 31, 2009. We also
        refer to your letter dated March 22, 2011, requesting amendments to the Agreement. We are
        pleased to inform you that the Bank concurs with your request, and proposes to amend the
        Agreement as follows:

        the table set forth in Attachment I to this Letter of Amendment.

        dating, and returning to us the enclosed copy of this Letter of Amendment. Upon receipt by the
        Bank of the copy of this Letter of Amendment countersigned by you, this Letter of Amendment will
        become effective as of the date of countersignature.
