                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Project implementation is almost completed with rehabilitation of selected Class I, II, and III roads across the country, totaling 307 km. Construction works have been completed on all
                         sections , including 117 kilometers of Class I roads, 127 kilometers of Class II roads, and 63 kilometers of Class III roads. A Road Management System has been developed under
                         the Project including all key elements: software, hardware, data, and, trained Bulgarian experts in the Road Institute and in RIA’s headquarters. The Road management and data
                         systems are used to develop budget-constrained programs for maintenance and rehabilitation of roads for the entire national road network using up-to-date data, to evaluate

                         are now in use for some motorways' sections. With the project restructuring the Institutional building component was expanded with new activities to help the government increase EU
                         funds absorption. In this respect, a highway adviser was hired to provide specific and ad-hoc support to RIA and MRDPW. A Road Safety Management Capacity Review has been
                         conducted and a Road Safety Improvement Action Plan developed. RIA has selected consultants to design solutions to improve traffic safety at 25 “black spots?. The detailed
