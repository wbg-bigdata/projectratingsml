                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The Project's Development Objective is to support national Government policy to: (i) improve the coverage, efficiency, and quality of the Argentine education
                         system, and (ii) to improve the governance of the Argentine education system through strengthening of thenormative, planning, information, monitoring, and
                         evaluation capacity at the national and provincial levels.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Following the Project Restructuring approved on September 1, 2011, implementation of the Project has advanced well, as reflected in the progress achieved in indicators. The
                         highlights for coverage and efficiency are as follows: with regards to coverage, the trend of 6 year olds in 1st grade living in rural areas and who did not attend preschool continues
                         to decline, reaching a low of 6,265 children in 2010. This compares very favorably to figures for 2009 (8,154) and since the baseline of 2004 (15,861), and even surpasses the end of

                         Key Decisions Regarding Implementation
                         In December 2010, the Federal Council of Education (the institution for setting national educational policy and strategy, encompassing all the provincial Ministers of Education)
                         approved the Document "Rural Education within that National Education System" as part of Resolution 128/10. This decision is the first policy step toward sustaining the rural
                         education modality in each province. The document provides the operational guidelines for this modality, in line with the current Federal Education Law.
