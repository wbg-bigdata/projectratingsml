                                   Amendment to the Financing Agreement

                    We refer to the Financing Agreement (the “Agreement?) between the Republic of
            Malawi (the “Recipient?) and the International Development Association (the
            “Association?) dated September 4, 2006, as amended to date, for the above mentioned
            Project. We also refer to your letter dated September 21, 2010, requesting the
            Association to amend the Agreement.

                   We are pleased to inform you that the Association concurs with your request and
            proposes to amend the above as follows:

            1.      The first paragraph of Schedule 1 to the Agreement is amended to read as follows:

                    “The objective of the Project is to improve access to basic infrastructure services
            in target areas.?

            2.      Section III.A.1(b) of Schedule 2 to the Agreement is amended to read as follows:

                    “(b) The performance indicators referred to above in sub-paragraph (a), which
                    shall be measured within the Project Area, consist of the following:

                             (i) Number of people provided with access to electricity under the
                                 Project by household connections;

                             (ii) Number of people provided with access to public phone services;

                             (iii) Number of people with access to improved water sources under the
                                   Project;

               (v) Proportion of rural population with access to an all season road.

               (vi) Proportion of female Project beneficiaries.?

3.     The table in Section V.A of Schedule 2 to the Agreement is amended to read as
