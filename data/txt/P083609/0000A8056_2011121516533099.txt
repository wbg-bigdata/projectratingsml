                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Overall project implementation is satisfactory. Safeguards and fiduciary activities are executed in a satisfactory manner. The performance of the project coordination unit also remains
                         satisfactory. Project progress has maintained a steady trend in the achievement of its original development objectives and expected outcomes. The target of 23,000 tons of fruits and
                         vegetables exports for 2009 has been achieved with 24,006 tons exported this year. Annual exports will reach 26,400 tons next cycle, thus in line with the 2010 target of 26,000 tons.
