   Solar Photovoltaic (PV) Program

India's PV program is among the largest in the
world with over 675,000 systems (50 MW )
installed, with domestic lighting and wat:r
pumping being prominent. India has a strong
manufacturing base with a production capacity
of 6.5 MW for cells (9 companies) and 9.5 MW
for modules (22 companies). There are 45
companies that manufacture balance-of
systems. India now exports PV modules to
countries in Asia and Europe.
