                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The project development objective (PDO) is to facilitate post-disaster economic recovery by improving the ability of the Governmentto work with poor rural
                         households in Nias to identify, develop and sustain livelihood opportunities.
