                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project has made important contributions to post-disaster economic recovery, improved livelihood opportunities for poor rural households, and more effective and sustainable
                         government support to rural communities. Community-level agricultural and women's livelihood groups were established as a key mechanism for empowering rural households,
                         identifying and conveying their needs and priorities, enhancing collaboration between communities and government, and ultimately for delivering training, grants, and agriculture
                         inputs and facilities to help them carry out the productive and livelihood development activities they chose themselves. In addition to investments in mainstream commercial products
