                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         To increase the access and mobility of Guiyang Municipality's transport users through priority infrastructure investments while establishing more sustainable
                         mechanisms for rural road maintenance, as well as enhancing capacity for keeping municipal debt under control.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project has met its Project Development Objectives. More than 95% of all civil works to improve access and mobility have been completed, a GIS-based rural road management
                         information system has been established to support improve sustained capacity to maintain rural roads, and a detailed advisory report has been prepared on supporting municipal
                         debt management to support increased debt-management capacity.
