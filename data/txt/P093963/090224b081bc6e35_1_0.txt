                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Component 1: Youxiao Road. Regarding resettlement, the Guiyang Municipal Government has formally committed the remaining funding required to complete the resettlement
                         activities. One of the two proposed resettlement sites, Shuitapo, has been substantially completed and is expected to be delivered before the end of 2013, and the other, on
                         Shachong Road, has received approvals for its planning and design and will undergo a land auction commencing in March 2013.

                         Component 2: Rural Transport. Rehabilitation of Zhuhua-Xinyang Road, which was damaged in a landslide, is still on-going. The “1 Road + 6 Stations? batch of projects is complete,
