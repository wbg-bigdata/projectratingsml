                                     Re: NICARAGUA-Credit No. 3760-NI
                                     (Off Grid Rural Electrification Project - PERZA)
                                     Sixth Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement


           Please refer to the Development Credit Agreement (the Credit Agreement), dated May 27, 2003,
    as amended on October 3, 2003, October 26, 2004, December 5, 2008, June 30, 2009 and July 14, 2010
    between the Republic of Nicaragua (the Borrower) and the International Development Association (the
    Association) for the above referenced Project. Terms not otherwise defined herein shall have the
    meanings assigned to them in the Credit Agreement.

            The Association has received the letter from the General Secretary of Nicaragua Ministry of
    Energy and Mines, requesting the amendment of the Credit Agreement for the purpose of reflecting the
    reallocation of proceeds among the Categories of the Credit Agreement.

           We are pleased to inform you that pursuant to the letter of request, the Bank hereby agrees to
    amend the Credit Agreement as follows:

    1.      Schedule 1. The table in Schedule 1 to the Credit Agreement is hereby amended to read as set
    forth in the Annex to this Amendment Letter.

             Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing amendment by signing, dating and returning to us
    the enclosed copy of this Amendment Letter. This Amendment Letter will be executed in two counterparts,
    each of which shall be an original. The provisions of this Amendment Letter will become effective upon the
    receipt by the Bank of the countersigned Amendment Letter as of the date of countersignature.
