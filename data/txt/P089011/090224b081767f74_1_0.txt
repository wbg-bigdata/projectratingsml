                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Project implementation continues to progress satisfactorily. Most planned activities and works have been completed. The Project is expected to close on December 31st, 2012 and up
                         to date, 95 percent of the loan has been disbursed. Sewerage collectors are being installed, in which out of 26km planned, so far 20km have been concluded. Works for micro and
                         macro drainage are about 50 percent executed and expected to be concluded by August 2013. Capacity building on environmental-related activities are being conducted by the
                         Municipality. Under the activities for conservation of natural resources, the system to monitor the quality of water in the region is being implemented.
