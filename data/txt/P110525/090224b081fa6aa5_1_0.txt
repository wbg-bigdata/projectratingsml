                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The MDTF has four months left for implementation (until the closing date of 31 December 2013). Thirteen out of fourteen of technical projects (TA) under the first implementation plan
                         (IP1) have been completed. Out of fifteen projects under IP2, two have been completed ( PEFA assessment and the development of the bond market roadmap until 2020) and two
                         (tax policy review and regulatory impact assessment -RIA) completed by 90%. The remaining nine projects are under implementation with various degree of advancement. Out of
                         which three needs special attention if they are to be completed on time. Since the last ISR, around 40 activities were approved to be added to the existing projects in IP2, most
