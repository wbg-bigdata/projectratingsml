                         Program Development Objectives
                         Program Development Objective (from Program Document)

                         To strengthen an inclusive, equitable and efficient social protection system by (1) promoting greater integration, efficiency and equity of social insurance, (2)
                         improving the targeting, efficiency, and articulation of social assistance programs, and (3) promoting higher quality training and employment services
                         Has the Program Development Objective been changed since Board Approval of the Program?

                            Yes           No

                         Implementation Status Overview
Public Disclosure Copy




                         The Government has achieved the measures defined in the DPL and remains highly committed to the reform agenda supported by this operation. Examples of outcomes and outputs
                         achieved include: i) improvements in the process of collection of social security contributions with an increase in the number of affiliates in the PILA to 9.5 million; ii) progress towards
                         the harmonization of the health insurance system through the approval in January of 2010 of the increase in the coverage of the subsidized regime for children under 18; iv) increase
                         in the number of families in extreme poverty attended by the national strategy Juntos to 1.2 million; and v) strengthening of the national training system through an increase in the
                         number of courses and institutions certified in terms of quality.
