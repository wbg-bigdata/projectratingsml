                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Overall, the project has recorded satisfactory progress towards achievement of its project objectives. The project has realized various outcomes from its sub-components, leading to
                         higher level impacts such increased number of beneficiaries with high levels of female participation; reduced student classroom ratios in subproject areas, increased the number of
                         people accessing basic services in health, improved water sources, improved sanitation, roads, and irrigation facilities; and employment creation. The project has also increased the
                         number of poor people involved in savings and investments.
