            Excellency:

                    We refer to the Financing Agreement (the Financing Agreement) between the Republic
            of Burundi (the Recipient) and the International Development Association (the Association) dated
            February 27, 2007 with respect to the above-captioned Project, and amended on November 2,
            2009. Please note that capitalized terms used in this letter (Second Amendment Letter) and not
            defined herein have the meaning ascribed to them in the Financing Agreement.

                    We also refer your letter dated November 22, 2010, requesting certain amendments to the
            Financing Agreement.

                 We are pleased to inform you that the Association proposes, through this Second
            Amendment Letter, to amend the following:

                    (1) Schedule 2, Section II.A. of the Financing Agreement as follows:

                “The Recipient shall monitor and evaluate the progress of the Project and prepare Project
            Reports in accordance with the provisions of Section 4.08 of the General Conditions and on the
            basis of the indicators acceptable to the Association. Each Project Report shall cover the period
            of one calendar quarter, and shall be furnished to the Association not later than 45 days after the
            end of the period covered by such report.?

                    (2) Schedule 2, Section IV.B of the Financing Agreement as follows:

                          “The Closing Date is June 30, 2012.?

                   Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing amendment by signing and dating this
            Second Amendment Letter in the spaces provided below. All other provisions of the Financing
            Agreement, except as amended herein, shall remain in full force and effect.
