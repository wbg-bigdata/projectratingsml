A. SUMMARY

The proposed change would, at the Borrower’s request and in fulfillment of the Project
Development Objective, extend the Closing Date of Loan 7628-BR to February 28, 2014.

B. PROJECT STATUS

The project is Moderately Satisfactory on both PDO and IP ratings. To date, 400
community subprojects have been approved for financing and implementation, benefiting
some 24,000 rural families. The performance of the Borrower is Satisfactory. The
rating for Financial Management, Procurement and Social and Environmental Safeguards
is also Satisfactory.

                                        ANNEX 3:
                                 Extension of Closing Date


            BRAZIL – PARAIBA SECOND RURAL POVERTY REDUCTION
                                 P104752
                                LN-7628-BR

                                   Restructuring Paper



1. The closing date for Brazil, Paraiba Second Rural Poverty Reduction, Loan 7628-BR,
   P104752 will be extended from February 28, 2013 until February 28, 2014.

2. The project is Moderately Satisfactory on both PDO and IP ratings. To date, 400
   community subprojects have been approved for financing and implementation,
   benefiting some 24,000 rural families. The performance of the Borrower is
   Satisfactory. The rating for Financial Management, Procurement and Social and
   Environmental Safeguards is also Satisfactory.

3. The proposed extension is necessary to allow the Borrower to continue carrying out
   the activities of the project, in compliance with the Project Development Objective.
   The Borrower has in place an action plan, acceptable to the Bank, for the completion
   of the project.

4. This will be the first extension of the project.
