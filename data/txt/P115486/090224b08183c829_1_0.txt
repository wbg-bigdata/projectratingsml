                         Overall Risk Rating                                                            Moderate

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         1. The LRIP implementation continues to progress in a satisfactory manner. All civil works activities planned under the original and LRIP-AF1, and several planned for LRIP-AF2, are
                         already completed. About 87% of Bank funds for the AF2 have been already disbursed and the AF1 closed in December 31, 2011.
                         2. The targets of the LRIP have now been met and even exceeded (incl. number of person-month of job created and number of km of roads rehabilitated). The second additional
                         financing to the LRIP is expected to close on December 31, 2013
                         3. The Transport PIU completed the recruiting exercise for additional staff. The capacity of the PIU to deliver on expected tasks and requests is very satisfactory.
                         4. The policy dialogue between the Bank and the Government on the issue of efficiency and sustainability in the road sector has been positive.
