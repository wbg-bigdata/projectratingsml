                           Re: BOLIVIA: GPOBA Grant Agreement TF090206-IFC, TF090207-DFID
                                    Decentralized Electricity for Universal Access Project
                                         Third Amendment to the Grant Agreement

            Excellency:

                    Please refer to the Grant Agreement dated June 26, 2007, as amended, between the
            Republic of Bolivia (“Recipient?) and the International Development Association (“World Bank?),
            acting as administrator of the Global Partnership on output-based Aid (“GPOBA?). In this letter
            (Amendment Letter) all terms defined in the Grant Agreement have the same meaning as therein set
            forth.

                     Pursuant to your request by letter No. MPD/VIPFE/DGGFE/UOF-000275/2011 040835
            dated Feb 3, 2011, as well as our discussions on the subject, we are pleased to inform you that the
            World Bank has established March 31, 2013 as the later date for purposes of Section IV.B.2 of
            Schedule 2 to the Grant Agreement. In addition, the World Bank hereby agrees to amend the Grant
            Agreement so as to: (a) decrease the length of the service contract for the solar home systems; and
            (b) reallocate the proceeds of the Grant among the disbursement Categories.

                    Consequently the Grant Agreement is amended as follows:

            1.      Section I.B.2 (e) (i) of Schedule 2 is amended to read in its entirety as follows:

                    “(i)      the obligation of the private sector service providers to: (A) market and install the
                              solar home systems; (B) provide satisfactory service to users for at least two (2)
                              years starting immediately after installations of the solar home systems; (C) carry
                              out at least one visit a year to each user; (D) provide specified market development
                              services; and (E) perform basic monitoring and evaluation tasks; and?

            2.       The table in Section IV.A.2 of Schedule 2 to the Grant Agreement is amended to read in its
            entirety as set forth in the attachment to this Amendment Letter.

                    All provisions of the Grant Agreement, except as amended herein, shall remain in full force
            and effect.
