                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Progress toward achieving the project s development objectives (PDO) is moderately satisfactory with four out of the five PDO output indicator targets already achieved and
                         surpassed. The key achievements include: (i) growth of registered sales transactions has increased by 56.9 %; (ii) improved efficiency in reduction in average processing time for
                         sales transaction, with a target time for registering a sales transaction in 0,5 days (4 hours); (iii) growth of registered mortgage transactions by at least 20 % above; (iv) number of real
                         estate units in the registry system has increased by 2.87 million; (v) creation of national IT system, formulation of strategic and business plans and proposal for Cadastral Service
                         Center.
