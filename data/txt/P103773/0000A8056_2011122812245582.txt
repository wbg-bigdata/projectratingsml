                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Progress towards achievement of the PDO has improved from Moderately Satisfactory to Satisfactory since the last mission, on account of the new refocused PDO that was put into
                         place as a result of the restructuring of 2011. Overall implementation progress remains satisfactory.

                         Following the signing of the Amended EU/World Bank administration Agreement in January 2011, a Grant Agreement between the Government of Malawi and the World Bank (as
                         administrator of the EU grant) was signed on 18 May, 2011 and EU co-financing has started being utilized to finance agreed activities. A second order restructuring (re-allocation of
                         proceeds) was also approved by the Acting Country Director in April 2011.
Public Disclosure Copy




                         Actual IDA expenditure to date, including commitments was at 97%. All EU funds will have to be committed by 31 December 2011. 50% of EU is yet to be disbursed but requests for
                         the 50% balance will have to be made in time to have resources available before the 31 December 2011 date.

                         All fiduciary aspects of the project are satisfactory. The project procurement and financial management are currently rated as satisfactory, and have been since project effectiveness.
                         Financial management is Satisfactory and the project is up-to-date with Interim Financial Reports (IFRs) as well as Audit Reports.
