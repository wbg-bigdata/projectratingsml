                         Implementation Status Overview
                         A mid-term review of BESTAP conducted in October 2010 rated the project as satisfactory. Disbursement of IDA resources is currently at 90%. All fiduciary aspects of the project are
                         satisfactory. The project procurement and financial management are currently rated as satisfactory, and have been since project effectiveness. Financial management is
                         Satisfactory and the project is up-to-date with Interim Financial Reports (IFRs) as well as Audit Reports. A level one restructuring of the Business Environment Technical Assistance

                         Project (BESTAP) was approved by the World Bank Board on a 10 day No Objection basis in November 2010. However, due to delays in finalizing administrative arrangements
                         needed to operationalize the use of European Union (EU) co-financing for activities that the World Bank and EU agreed would be jointly financed, there has been subsequent

                         overruns in actual disbursements over allocated disbursements in a number of disbursement categories where there was to be joint financing. In order to normalize this situation, a
                         second order restructuring for Country Director approval is being processed whose purpose is to to normalize this situation by reallocating proceeds among the relevant affected
                         categories. A revised EU/World Bank Administration Agreement has now been signed by both parties and a grant agreement has also been prepared for processing.
