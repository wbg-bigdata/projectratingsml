              I refer to the grant agreement (“Grant Agreement?), dated February 6, 2007, as amended
      through March 1, 2011, between the Republic of Indonesia (“Recipient) and the International Bank
      for Reconstruction and Development/International Development Association (the “World Bank?)
      acting as administrator of grant funds provided by multiple donors under the Java Reconstruction
      Trust Fund (“JRF?, TF070639) for the Community-Based Settlement Rehabilitation and
      Reconstruction Project for Central and West Java and Yogyakarta Special Region (the “Project?).
      Capitalized terms used but not defined in this letter have the meanings given to them in the Grant
      Agreement.

              The Grant Agreement provided for a grant in the amount of US$30,000,000 with the
      possibility of a subsequent grant of US$30,000,000 which was duly provided by amendment on
      December 7, 2007. A first Additional Financing grant of US$6,420,000 was provided on November
      16, 2009, with the possibility of a subsequent grant of US$5,200,000, which was duly provided by
      amendment on April 12, 2010. The total Grant amount provided as of April 12, 2010 was
      US$71,620,000. On March 1, 2011, a Second Additional Financing Grant of US$1,700,000 was
      provided, with the possibility of a subsequent grant not to exceed US$1,800,000 and an extension of
      the Closing Date of the Grant Agreement to June 30, 2012, subject to extension of the JRF. The total
      Grant amount provided as of March 1, 2011 was US$73,320,000. The extension of the JRF has been
      duly accomplished.

                   Accordingly:

      1.           This is to inform you that, in accordance with paragraph 1(c) of the Additional Financing
                   Amendment, the Bank has allocated an additional amount of one million eight hundred
                   thousand United States Dollars (US$1,800,000) in the form of a Subsequent Additional
                   Financing grant to assist in financing the Project.
