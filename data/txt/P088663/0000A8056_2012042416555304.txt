                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The objectives of the Project are to (i) expand and enhance the family medicine model of primary health care; (ii) build managementcapacity in the sector; and (iii)
                         strengthen the policy making process through the development and implementation of a system for monitoring and evaluating sector performance.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The Additional Financing (AF) was declared effective on December 27, 2011. All activities are completed with the original credit being fully disbursed (closed on December 31, 2011).
                         Neither the Federation (FBiH) nor the Republika Srpska (RS) has open their respective designated account to make the first disbursement under the AF. However, both entities are
                         implementing the activities using counterpart funds according to their respective procurement plans.

                         The expansion and restructuring of the primary health care system based on the family medicine (FM) model is on track. The project has supported the development and
