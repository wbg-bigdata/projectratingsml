                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The Restructuring of the project has been approved by the World Bank Country Director and the amendment to the project's legal agreement countersigned by the Minister of
                         Finance on March 20th, 2012. The aim of the restructuring was to: (i) revise the PDOs; (ii) modify the institutional arrangements; (iii) revise disbursement categories; and (iv) extend
                         the closing date to December 30th, 2013. The project made its first disbursement on March 30th, 2012. The project action plan for 2012 has been finalized and approved. Activities to
                         implement project has started.
