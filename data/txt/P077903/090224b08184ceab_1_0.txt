                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Under the original grant, 88.9 percent (US$7,593,072) of the total grant amount of US$8 million and under the Additional Financing 60.9 percent (US$3,436,390) of the total AF of US
                         $5.5 million have been disbursed as of November 15, 2012. Most activities have beencompleted with the remaining ones including the construction of two basic cycle schools, eight
                         multi-grade schools, and continuous in-service teacher training.
