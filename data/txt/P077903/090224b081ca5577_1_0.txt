                         Implementation Status Overview
                         To date, more than 92% (US$7,836,367) of the total grant amount of US$8 million and almost 89% (US$4,963,096) of the total AF of US$5.5 million have been disbursed as of May
                         2013. Most activities have been completed with the remaining ones to include: the construction of two basic cycle schools and continuous in-service teacher training for primary
                         teachers in Math and English including through e-learning as the installment of equipment in regions and all cluster schools will be fully completed before the next training session
                         scheduled for July 2013.
