                         Implementation Status Overview
                         A review of the action plan agreed upon during the mid-term review mission demonstrated that the Project is progressing satisfactorily. The Project reached the Project Development
                         Objective (PDO) indicators. From now until the Project's close on March 31, 2013, OSE will focus its efforts on completing works at the Aguas Corrientes Water Treatment Plant
