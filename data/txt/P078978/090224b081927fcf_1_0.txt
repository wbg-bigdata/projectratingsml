                         Project Development Objectives
                         Community & Basic Health Project (P078978)
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)
                         The PDO is to increase access to, utilization of, and patient satisfaction with health services in project-supported areas; and to build capacity and efficiency at national
                         and oblast and rayon levels in administering the basic benefit package and per capita financing for primary health care.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project is in the final month of implementation and all project activities are either completed or will be completed prior to the closing date of December 31, 2012. Project
                         disbursement has reached 93%, and the remaining funds are committed, waiting for payments to be made within the four months grace period till end April 2013. Due to the

                         exchange rate changes, the project is expected to gain 3% (US$94,861), making the total grant amount reaching US$3,094,861. The proposed plan is to use the extra 3% for project
                         management, including payments for moving the PIU office to the MOH premises and for printing of health facility management manuals and related documents for health workers in
                         the project districts. Progress towards achievements of PDO is assessed as Moderately Satisfactory, and the overall implementation of the project is rated Satisfactory.
