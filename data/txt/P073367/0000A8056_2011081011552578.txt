                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The implementation of the IDTR Project finished on May 27, 2011. Important results were achieved: (a) the installation of about 10,170 solar home systems benefiting more than
Public Disclosure Copy




                         40,000 people and 134 Solar Home Systems in schools and clinics benefiting 30,000 people; (b) the provision of public lighting to 20,000 people; (c) 20,070 rural electricity
                         connections through the extension of the electricity grid connections (densification) and (d) 7649 improved cookstoves.
